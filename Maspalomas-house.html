Surf Camp Maspalomas

Our house, a beautiful two storey beach house is situated in Maspalomas, the islands biggest and most popular beach which is so vast that it rarely feels crowded. Along with the adjacent Playa del Inglés beach it is over 6 kilometres long with calm water and huge golden dunes stretching back for as far as the eye can see. Newcomers be warned though - the famous nudist section is in the middle of the beach next to the biggest dunes, so if you do wander off you might end up in the midst of it all!

The town itself has excellent nightlife and all the usual amenities perfect for exploring after a long day of surfing such as shops, restaurants and bars. The locals are incredibly friendly, and we are happy to offer some recommendations. We look forward to welcoming you!

Prices:

The price for the duration of 7 nights with 6 days of surf school is €525. If you wish to stay longer, an option with 14 nights accommodation and 12 days of school costs 1000€. If you have any other special requests, just send us an email on info@wipeoutsurfcamp.com and we will see how we can help you.

If you are at a beginner level, we would recommend that you stay longer than one week in the camp as your skills will improve greatly in the second week.
